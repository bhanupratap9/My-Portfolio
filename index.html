How I Work

My QA Process

A structured approach that catches bugs early and keeps your release cycle fast and reliable.

01
Discovery & Planning
Understand requirements, define scope, write a test strategy and test plan tailored to your product and risks.
02
Test Case Design
Write clear, reusable test cases covering functional, edge, and negative scenarios. Documented in JIRA or your preferred tool.
03
Execution & Automation
Manual exploratory testing + Selenium with Java automation suites + Postman API collections. Integrated into your CI/CD pipeline.
04
Bug Reporting
Detailed bug reports with severity, reproducible steps, screenshots, and environment info. Clear enough for any developer to fix fast.
05
Sign-off & Handover
Final test summary report, regression pass confirmation, and handover of all test assets for your team to maintain.
